About Me

Background

While working in my family's printary company, I discovered that layout calculation was one of the most time-consuming tasks in the printary process, so I created the layout calculator to speed up the process.

My Role

• Solution / UX Design

• VBA Coding

Year

2019

Problem

Calculate cuting layout take so much time

Every time the printery is required to cut paper to an unusual size, the person in charge of cutting the paper must calculate the most efficient way to cut the paper in order to get the most paper sheet out of it. Depending on the complexity of the layout, the process can take anywhere from 3 to 10 minutes.

Goal

Create a simple and easy to use layout calculator

I believe that the layout calculation process can be automated using the Excel VBA coding, the image below is my first concept of the calculator.

Test + Improvement

Functional > Simplicity

After testing the first iteration of the calculator with the user, he stated that the calculator is useful, but it should include more functionality, such as having a paper size preset, a recommended cutting size, a remanding paper calculation, and an unit conversion converter. By the end, the calculator is no longer simple but very functional.

Result

Reduce the process work by around 20 hours per month

• More accurate layout cutting
